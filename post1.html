Let's Visit Covent Garden Market

Header

Located at 130 King St is the Covent Garden Market At a seriosly close walking distance from my school I was surprised that I had not been there before. I'm happy to say this review is extremely authentic. I was surprised at how huge the entire market was. It's large and all the stores are packed closely together but it has a very cozy feeling. At my first time there I got lost while looking for a store with collectibles. There are so many food options with unique recipes like macarons, food you can take to.

Header

What makes the Market extra special is that many of the vendors have hand-made items. Moat of these are also very good for the environment so you will be sipporting your local vendors and taking care of planet earth! that's a win win for me! I saw various keychains, scarfs, randon knickknacks, figurines and very well made cute stuffed toys. There's definately atleast one thing you will want to buy here!

Header

Finally I arrived at Forget Me Not Flowers and More. You see, I worked at Home Depot in the summer of 2019 in their garden department so I absolutely adore flowers and succelents the fake ones of course! The vendor was especially friendly and you could tell the flowers had a been given a lot of care and love. The most you can spend here is atleast $15! and you will be going home with a nice little therapeutic succelent and bamboo family!
