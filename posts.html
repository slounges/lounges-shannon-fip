Welcome To March's Top 3!

This month's theme was collectables and I visited three places in Downtown London in search for some items I would love to add to or start a collection with! The places I blogged about this time were: The Covent Market, Attic Books and Heroes Comics all located in the heart of Downtown! Better yet, they were all in close proximity to one another and are definatley worth the visit!

Read On to find out why!

This lovely book store is located on 240 Dundas St. It has a wide range of genres. Just like the name suggests, it is a book store but with a more "attic like" prescence. Just like an attic they carry old, historic, used and donated pieces of literature!
