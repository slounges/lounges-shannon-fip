Welcome to London Eye!

Have you ever found yourself bored on a day off?
This happens to me too!

It's always good and sometimed fun to discover new things. What if you made it a habit to support your local shops and businesses atleast once a month when you felt this way? Not only would you be benefiting London,but it would get you and your family out of the house! Don't know where exactly to go? You're in the right place. London, Ontario has many interesting stores right outside your door! whether a restaurant, comic shop or old library the London Eye Blog was specifically created to tell you everything you need to know about various places to go in London! This blog follows me as I visit 3 places every month, take a few pictures and give my overall review! Every place is guaranteed to be family friendly and lots of fun! In the month of March I went to a few places downtown London and picked out my top 3!
